How can I tell if my resume is ATS compliant?

Running your resume through an ATS-compliant resume checker is the best way to determine whether it meets the tracking system requirements. Whether you upload an existing resume or build a new one from scratch, our resume scanner will help you improve your resume's performance.

What is a good ATS score for a resume?

A good score on an ATS resume scan is 80% or higher. This score will typically move your resume to a recruiter, who will review it and decide whether or not to contact you for an interview.

How can I check my resume score for free?

MyPerfectResume can check resumes for free and identify common errors, including misspellings, formatting issues and missing keywords or sections.

Which resume file format is best to use for ATS?

ATS software will accept most file formats, including PDF and Word, if they use layout, fonts and content optimized for parsing. Use easy-to-read fonts for your resume, such as Times New Roman, Arial or Helvetica.
